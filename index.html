About Us

We are Axel and Julián, founders of Reprocard and The Pot Club, cannabis-related projects based in Argentina. Both graduates in International Trade, with over 10 years of experience in cannabis cultivation and authors of the thesis "Exportation of Medicinal Cannabis to Chile for the Refractory Epilepsy Segment."

Axel

Bachelor in International Trade, passionate about technology. Currently focused on developing control and monitoring solutions for cultivation environments, as well as designing automation processes to optimize cultivation, traceability, and dispensing systems.

Julián

Bachelor in International Trade, also working at a leading company that provides data capture, automatic identification, and traceability solutions. He has over 10 years of experience in cannabis cultivation.

Together, they worked on their undergraduate thesis titled "Exportation of Medicinal Cannabis to Chile for the Refractory Epilepsy Segment" and developed REPROCARD, an e-commerce platform for cannabis-related services and products, initially offering the printing service for the official REPROCANN physical credential (Argentina’s National Cannabis Producer Registry), as well as The Pot Club, a civil association operating under Argentina’s Law 27.350 for medicinal cannabis, focused on achieving the highest standards in cultivation and production processes.

Reprocard

Reprocard is an e-commerce platform for high-quality cannabis industry services and products. We aim to offer everything we have tried in our facilities that provides superlative quality.

Reprocard started as a service for printing the physical ID card for the REPROCANN program certificate, reaching to print and send over 11,000 cards across the country.

The Pot Club

The Pot Club operates as a civil association under Argentina's Law 27.350 for medicinal cannabis. We currently cultivate cannabis for REPROCANN patients, striving for the highest quality standards.

We work with top brands that support every stage of our cultivation processes, including Athena, PulseGrow, Plagron and BlueLab.

In addition, we develop our own solutions to enhance process efficiency and automation for monitoring and controlling cultivation variables.

Aplicamos los protocolos de la línea ATHENA con precisión, integrando riego automatizado y monitoreo de variables como pH, EC, VPD y humedad del sustrato. Cada dato es registrado y visualizado para mejorar continuamente el rendimiento.

Esta sinergia entre tecnología y nutrición vegetal nos posiciona como socios ideales para representar oficialmente la marca ATHENA en la región.
